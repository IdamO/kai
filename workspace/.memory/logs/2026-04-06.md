## 23:30 [MILESTONE] Feature Predictor v2 — R²=0.89 Loudness, 0.87 Energy
MASSIVE improvement over v1 (R²≈0 across all features).

**Root cause of v1 failure:** L2 normalization across the embedding vector destroyed amplitude-dependent signals. Energy, loudness, and acousticness all depend on signal magnitude, which L2 normalization flattens to unit sphere.

**Fix:** Per-dimension standardization (subtract mean, divide by std per dimension). Preserves relative amplitude patterns while stabilizing training.

**Results (99645 tracks, layers 18+19, 2048d, best at epoch 5):**
- Loudness: R²=0.889 | Energy: R²=0.866 | Acousticness: R²=0.874
