Three findings that reshape the A/B scope vs the queue-item spec:
1. **N=20 is severely underpowered.** Even at 30% baseline re-share rate, 20 vs 20 needs a 40pp absolute lift (2.4x fold) to detect at 80% power. Downgrade P1 from "falsification A/B" to "N=20 directional pilot" — prior-updating Bayesian exercise, not frequentist test.
